BUDGET FRIED RICE

A simple and affordable fried rice made with fluffy rice, scrambled eggs, and mixed vegetables, all tossed together with savory soy sauce for a quick and satisfying meal.

Cooking time 20-30 minutes

Serves 2-3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old rice works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil (vegetable or canola)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Optional
  • Green onions
  • Chicken

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over medium heat..
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the frozen vegetables. Cook for 3–4 minutes until heated through.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and stir well.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and mix everything together
  6. Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and stir to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2–3 minutes, then serve warm.
