FETTUCCINE ALFREDO

A simple, creamy pasta dish made with just a few ingredients.

Cooking time: 20–40 minutes

Serves: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Save ½ cup pasta water.
  3. Cook garlic in butter.
  4. Add cream and simmer.
  5. Mix in Parmesan until smooth.
  6. Add pasta and combine.
  7. Season and serve.
