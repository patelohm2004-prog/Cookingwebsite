FETTUCCINE ALFREDO

A simple, creamy pasta dish made with just a few ingredients — perfect for beginners and busy college students.

Cooking time 20-40 minutes

Serves 2-3

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook according to the package directions (about 10–12 minutes).
  2. Before draining the pasta, save ½ cup of the pasta water. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly. Do not let it brown.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and slowly stir in the Parmesan cheese. Mix until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water until it reaches your desired consistency.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss until the noodles are fully coated in the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately. Garnish with parsley if desired.
