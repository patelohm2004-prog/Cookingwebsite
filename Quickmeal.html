GRILLED CHEESE & TOMATO SOUP

A warm, comforting classic made with crispy grilled cheese and creamy tomato soup — perfect for beginners, busy college students, or anyone who wants a quick and satisfying meal.

Cooking time 15-20 minutes

Serves 2

Ingredients For The Grilled Cheese

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese (American, cheddar, or mozzarella)
  • 2 tbsp butter

Ingredients For The Tomato Soup

  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 cup milk (or water, check can instructions)

Instructions

  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat. Pour in the tomato soup and milk (or water). Stir well.
  2. Cook the soup for about 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through. Turn heat to low and keep warm.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place one slice of bread butter-side down in a pan over medium heat. Add 2 slices of cheese on top.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes until golden brown. Flip and cook the other side until golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat for the second sandwich.
  8. Slice the sandwiches in half and serve with the warm tomato soup.
